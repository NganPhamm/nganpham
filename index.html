Jin


Kim Seok-jin, commonly known by his stage name Jin, is a South Korean male singer and songwriter. He is a member of the South Korean boy band BTS. Jin co-wrote and released 3 solo songs with BTS: "Awake", "Epiphany" and "Moon", all of which debuted on Korea's Gaon Digital Chart.

v


Kim Tae-hyung, commonly known by his stage name V, is a South Korean male singer and songwriter. He is a member of the South Korean boy band BTS.

Jung-kook


Jeon Jung-kook, commonly known by his stage name Jungkook, is a South Korean male singer and songwriter. He is the youngest member and the main vocalist of the South Korean boy band BTS.

RM


Kim Nam-joon, commonly known by his stage name RM, is a South Korean rapper, songwriter, and music producer. He is the leader of the South Korean boy band BTS. In 2015, he released his first solo mixtape RM.

Sugar


Min Yoon-gi, commonly known by the stage names Suga and Agust D, is a South Korean rapper, songwriter, and music producer. He is a member of the South Korean boy band BTS. In 2016, he released his debut solo mixtape Agust D.

Jimin


Park Ji-min, commonly known by his stage name Jimin, is a South Korean singer, songwriter and dancer. He is a member of the South Korean boy band BTS.

J-hope


Jung Ho-seok, commonly known by his stage name J-Hope, is a South Korean rapper, songwriter, dancer and music producer. He is a member of the South Korean boy band BTS. J-Hope released his debut mixtape Hope World in 2018.
